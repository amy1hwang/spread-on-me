Butter Of December

Peppermint Butter

Ingredients

Holiday season is back! Spread On Me's Peppermint Butter, is made with fresh peppermint herbs, crushed candy canes, and butter from organically grass-fed cows. This butter is delicious with waffles, scones, pancakes, toast, hot chocolate, and as a substitute for plain butter when baking, or to make frosting/glazes.
